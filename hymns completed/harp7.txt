(Translation : Christ Heals yes) hymn 7 – Cristo Cura Sim

Brazillian version :

1
  Contra os males deste mundo,
  Deus nos vale só;
  Não há mal que Deus não cure,
  Pois de nós tem dó.

    Refrão
      Cristo cura, sim,
      Cristo cura, sim.
      Seu amor por nós é Imenso;
      Ele cura, sim!


2
  Derramou Seu sangue puro
  P'ra remir a mim;
  Quando ungido sou de azeite,
  Sou curado, enfim!

3
  Só noss’alma é bem segura,
  Oculta em Jesus;
  Ele o bálsamo da vida,
  Derramou na cruz.

4
  Glória a Deus! Eterna glória,
  Demos-Lhe louvor;
  Glória, cânticos e hosanas
  Dai ao Redentor!


Original version :

1 
  When I fear my faith will fail,
  Christ will hold me fast;
  When the tempter would prevail,
  He can hold me fast!

 Chorus
    He will hold me fast,
    He will hold me fast;
    For my Savior loves me so,
    He will hold me fast.

2 
  I could never keep my hold,
  He must hold me fast;
  For my love is often cold,
  He must hold me fast. [Refrain]

3 
  I am precious in His sight,
  He will hold me fast;
  Those He saves are His delight,
 He will hold me fast. [Refrain]

4 
  He'll not let my soul be lost,
  Christ will hold me fast;
  Bought by Him at such a cost,
  He will hold me fast. [Refrain]

  Amen.


Changes ( *if any) :

All the lyrics are changed, also the ``Amen´´ was removed, The focus of the lyrics on both versions is on Jesus Christ.
